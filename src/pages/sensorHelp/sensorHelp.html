Sensor Help Page Presence Sensor The presence sensors are passive infrared sensors (PIR). They are mounted on the ceiling in the middle of the room. They detect motion in an angle of 110 degree. Air Sensor The gas sensor measures lot of different values and calculates an percentage over all. 100% is the best air quality, 0% is worst value. Each sensor has be calibrated manually therefor the result may variate. Noise Sensor The noise sensor measures sound waves in percentages of maximum calibration. 100% is the worst value, 0% is quietest. Each sensor has be calibrated manually therefor the result may variate. But you can assume that 100% correspond to about 120 decibel.
