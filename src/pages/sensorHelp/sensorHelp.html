Presence Value

detected - not detected

The presence sensors are passive infrared sensors (PIR). They are mounted on the ceiling in the middle of the room. They detect motion in an angle of 110 degree.

Air Quality

0 - 100% best at 100%

The gas sensor measures different values like humidity, pressure and gas resistance and calculates an percentage over all. 100% is the best air quality, 0% is worst. Each sensor has be calibrated manually therefor the results may variate.

Noise Level

0 - 100% best at 0%

The noise sensor measures sound waves in percentages of maximum calibration in average of 3 seconds. 100% is the loudest, 0% is quietest. Each sensor has be calibrated manually therefor the results may variate. You can assume that 100% correspond to about 100 decibel.
